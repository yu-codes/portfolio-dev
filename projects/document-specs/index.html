Overview

DocSpecs is a comprehensive project documentation specification toolkit covering 25+ deliverable documents across the entire software development lifecycle. It provides standardized templates with clear section structures and content guidelines — from pre-project proposals and contracts, through requirements analysis, system design, testing and acceptance, to deployment and maintenance. Designed for direct use as company SOPs or client-facing deliverables.

Key Features

  • Full SDLC Coverage: 8 lifecycle phases from proposal to maintenance, each with dedicated document templates
  • Independent & Deliverable: Every document stands alone as a complete, signable deliverable
  • Clear Section Structure: Each template defines specific sections, content guidelines, and sign-off pages
  • Practical Focus: Covers contract terms (IP, NDA, SLA), risk management (DR/BCP), and change control workflows
  • Ready-to-Use: Can be directly adopted as internal SOP or customized for client deliverables
  • Disaster Recovery & Security: Includes RTO/RPO planning, security policies, and audit mechanisms
